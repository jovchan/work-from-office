About Us

Our Company

Founded in 2023, WORK FROM OFFICE, a product of PT ARC Properti Indonesia, stands at the forefront of revolutionizing the property industry. With a unique focus on helping property owners and landlords maximize their vacant office spaces, we pride ourselves on delivering unparalleled value to these properties. Boasting over a decade of experience in the property sector, our founders bring a wealth of knowledge and an extensive network, encompassing both clients and fellow property agencies. This invaluable foundation empowers us to create opportunities that benefit both property owners and discerning professionals seeking the perfect workspace.

Backed by our established platforms, sewakantoronline.com and sewakantorupdate.com, WORK FROM OFFICE possesses an expansive and dynamic client database. This wealth of resources positions us as a market leader, uniquely poised to match vacant office spaces with the right tenants. Our commitment to excellence is not only reflected in the breadth of our connections, but also in our dedication to providing a seamless, user-friendly experience. With WORK FROM OFFICE, property owners can trust that their spaces will not only be occupied, but optimized for success, while professionals can find their ideal workspace with ease. We are dedicated to reshaping the way we approach property rentals, and with our proven track record, we are poised to make a lasting impact on the industry.

As we forge ahead, our mission remains clear: to be the driving force behind successful property matches, creating spaces where businesses thrive and professionals flourish.

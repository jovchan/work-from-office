Privacy Of Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how ARC Property Company collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our property website, Work From Office. By accessing or using the Website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide: We may collect information that you provide when using the Website, including when you create a user account, submit property inquiries, or communicate with us. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information: We may automatically collect certain information when you visit the Website, including your IP address, device information, browser type, and usage data. We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect this information.

1.3 Information from Third Parties: We may receive information about you from third parties, such as property owners, agents, or service providers, in connection with your use of the Website.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 We may use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Website and its features.
  • To communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • To personalize and improve your experience on the Website.
  • To send you updates, newsletters, or marketing materials, where permitted by law and with your consent.
  • To enforce our Terms of Use and other legal agreements.
  • To protect the security and integrity of the Website.


3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 We may share your information with:

  • Property owners, agents, or other users of the Website in connection with property inquiries or transactions.
  • Service providers and vendors who assist us in providing and improving the Website.
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Third parties in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets.


4. Your Choices

4.1 You can control and manage certain aspects of your information:

  • You can update or correct your account information at any time.
  • You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the instructions provided in those communications.


5. Security

5.1 We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Links and Services

6.1 The Website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these websites or services before providing them with your information.

7. Children's Privacy

7.1 The Website is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so that we can promptly delete it.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

8.1 We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the revised Privacy Policy on the Website.

9. Contact Us

9.1 If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at marketing@arcproperty.id

By using the Website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
